About ISTS

The International School of Technology & Sciences, popularly known as ISTS women's college of engineering, was established in the year 2009 to empower the women pudding engineers with technical and entrepreneurial knowledge and skills and inspire them to contribute to the socio-economic augmentation of the nation.
The college is located near Rajamahendravaram on NH.16. East Gonagudem, Rajanagaram, E.G.Dist, A.P., and is well connected by Road, Rail, and
The International School of Technology & Sciences affiliated to JNTUK approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
